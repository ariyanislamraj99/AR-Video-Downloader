About

Glass Video Downloader detects and downloads direct video sources (MP4, WebM, MKV, MOV, OGG) and HLS/DASH playlists (M3U8/MPD) from any webpage using DOM inspection and network request monitoring.

For platforms that use encryption or signature ciphers (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter), in-browser extraction is not technically possible without a server. The extension hands those off to your configured external helper.

HLS/DASH streams are surfaced as playlist URLs — copy them into a tool like yt-dlp, VLC, or ffmpeg to capture the full video, since Chrome's download API cannot merge segmented streams.
